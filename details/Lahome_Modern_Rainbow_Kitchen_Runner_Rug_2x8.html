Lahome Modern Rainbow Kitchen Runner Rug

This vibrant kitchen rug runner combines style and practicality, making it the perfect choice for any modern or farmhouse space. Its striking rainbow design adds a touch of color and visual appeal to your kitchen, hallway, or entryway.
